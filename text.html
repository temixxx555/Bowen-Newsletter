Bowen's New VC:Dive into a World of Unveiled Secrets and Extraordinary Journey's

What you need to know about The new VC

Published every Friday's

by Tech Bro's

The vice chancellor of Bowen University is Professor Jonathan Oyebamiji Babalola, a professor of Biophysical Chemistry, with over 30 years of research, teaching and administrative experience³. He is the 5th substantive vice chancellor of Bowen, which is a licensed private institution owned by the Nigerian Baptist Convention⁵. The motto of the university is "Excellence and Godliness"⁵.

Professor Babalola has made significant contributions to knowledge in hemoglobin reactivities, lipid transfer and membrane fusion, lipid nanodiscs, electrochemistry, electrocatalysis and adsorption chemistry³. He has 136 publications, which include 111 journal articles, 15 edited books, 4 book chapters, 4 conference proceedings and 2 technical reports, and has supervised multiple PhD theses and M.Sc research projects³.!

He is a recipient of the Alexander von Humboldt Fellowship, and a senior associate of The Abdus Salam International Centre for Theoretical Physics, Trieste, Italy since 2017³. He is a Fellow of the Nigeria Academy of Science, a Fellow of the Royal Society of Chemistry (UK), a Fellow of the Chemical Society of Nigeria, a Fellow of the Safety Training Programme of the Committee on Chemical Industry of the International Union of Pure and Applied Chemistry (IUPAC), a Pilot Associate of the Organisation for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapon, The Hague, Netherlands and a Justice of the Peace of Oyo State, Nigeria³.

Professor Babalola started his academic journey at the University of Ibadan, where he obtained his B.Sc (Hons) in Chemistry in 1984, and his M.Sc and PhD in Physical Chemistry in 1987 and 1991, respectively

He joined the Department of Chemistry of the University of Ibadan as a Graduate Assistant in 1987, and rose through the ranks to become a Professor in 2003³. He served as the Head of Department of Chemistry from 2009 to 2011, and the Dean of the Faculty of Science from 2011 to 2015³. He was also the pioneer Provost of the Post Graduate College of the University of Ibadan from 2015 to 2019¹. He has been involved in several national and international collaborations, such as the African Network for Solar Energy (ANSOLE), the International Centre for Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology (ICGEB), the International Foundation for Science (IFS), and the Third World Academy of Sciences (TWAS)³.

Babalola is succeeding professor olalekan ogunwole,whose tenure expires on Tuesday,August 1 2023.

Professor Babalola was appointed as the new vice chancellor of Bowen University on April 28, 2023, after a rigorous selection process by the Governing Council of the University¹. He assumed office on August 1, 2023, succeeding Professor Joshua Ogunwole, who completed his tenure on July 31, 2023⁵. Professor Babalola has a vision to transform Bowen University into a world-class institution that will produce graduates who are academically excellent, morally upright, socially responsible, and globally relevant³. He is also committed to fostering a culture of innovation, entrepreneurship, and community engagement among the staff and students of the University

He is married to Dr. (Mrs.) Olubunmi Babalola, a lecturer in the Department of Educational Management of the University of Ibadan, and they are blessed with three children³.

What you need to know about The chaplain

Published every Friday's

by Tech Bro's

Revd. Dr. Gideon Akanbi is the chaplain of Bowen University, a private Baptist Christian Nigerian university located in Iwo, Osun State. He is also an educator, an author, a life coach, a Christian entrepreneur, a business consultant and a speaker³. He has a passion for inspiring and informing people, both young and old, to realize their true potential³.

According to his website³, he was born in Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria. He attended the University of Ibadan, where he obtained a Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics. He later proceeded to the University of Lagos, where he earned a Master of Science degree in Statistics. He also holds a Doctor of Philosophy degree in Statistics from the same university.

He started his career as a lecturer in the Department of Statistics, University of Lagos, where he taught for over 10 years. He later joined the faculty of Bowen University in 2012, where he rose to the rank of Associate Professor of Statistics. He also served as the Head of Department of Mathematics and Statistics from 2014 to 2018.

He became the chaplain of Bowen University in 2018, where he oversees the spiritual affairs of the university community

He is also the founder and president of Gideon Akanbi Ministries, a non-denominational Christian organization that is committed to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ and empowering people to live a fulfilled life³.He is the author of several books, such as "The Force of Knowledge", "Grace for Maximized Living", "The Power of Vision", "The Secrets of Success", and "The Principles of Leadership". He is also a regular speaker at various conferences, seminars, workshops, and church programs, both locally and internationally³.
